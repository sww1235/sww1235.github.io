Circuit Analysis [#circuit-analysis}

The first step in this type of circuit analysis I have found, is to attempt to simplify the circuit as much as possible. This means:

  • remove any jumpers in circuit
  • add in ground and power symbols to reduce clutter
  • attempt to identify common circuit elements - Utilize the manual for the device you are reverse engineering if possible. This can give clues about certain portions of the circuit.

A Non Exhaustive List of Common Circuit Elements

  • darlington pair, with or without base-emitter bypass resistor
  • op amps
